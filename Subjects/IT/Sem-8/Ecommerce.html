E Commerce Syllabus

1.Introduction: definition of electronic commerce, e-commerce: technology and prospects, incentives for engaging in electronic commerce, needs of e-commerce, advantages and disadvantages, framework, Impact of E-commerce on business, E-Commerce Models.
2.Network Infrastructure for E- commerce: Internet and intranet based E-commerce- Issues, problems and prospects, Network infrastructure, network access equipments, Broadband telecommunication (ATM, iSdn, frame Relay).
3.Mobile commerce: introduction, W ireless application protocol, wap technology, mobile Information device.
4.Web security: security issues on web, importance of firewall, components of firewall, transaction security, Emerging client server, Security threats, network security, factors to consider in firewall design, Limitation of Firewalls.
5. Encryption: encryption techniques, symmetric encryption: keys and data encryption standard, Triple encryption, secret key encryption; asymmetric encryption: public and private pair key encryption, Digital Signatures, virtual private Network.
6.Electronic payments: overview, the set protocol, payment gateway, certificate, digital Tokens, smart card, credit card, magnetic strip card, e-checks, credit/debit card based EPS, online Banking.
7. EDI application in business, e- commerce law, forms of agreement, govt. Policies and Agenda.
